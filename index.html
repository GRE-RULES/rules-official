💡 Tips for Best Results with the AI Assistant:
  • Be the Player: Use first-person phrasing (e.g., "I" or "my opponent") instead of abstract terms like Player A and Player B.
  • State Cause First: Explain why an action occurred before asking for the penalty (e.g., "I lifted my ball because my opponent gave me the wrong score.").
  • One Step at a Time: Break complex scenarios down. Ask about the immediate infraction before explaining subsequent events.
